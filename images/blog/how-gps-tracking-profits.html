Fleet Tracking Solutions

So first off, can you save money for the company? Well, what costs you money with your fleet? Fuel, maintenance and service costs? Add to that driver error – bad driving habits and insurance busting accidents. All of these things can add significant expense to your bottom line. But is there really no way to control them? For many companies wasted money will become budgeted expenses, how crazy is that?

Arguably fuel is a no brainer expense, you have to have fuel to run your vehicles. True, but could you spend less on fuel and have more money for something else? Absolutely, and here’s an example of how. With easy installation you get GPS tracking up and running in a vehicle. With One Step GPS we get you set up with unreal data access. Real-time tracking shows how fast the vehicle is going and how your driver is accelerating and decelerating. In other words you can know where a driver is putting excessive strain on the engine, leading to decreased fuel economy and brake and engine repairs on the horizon. You can set alerts for unsafe events, straight to your phone.

This kind of driver error is easy to correct. In fact many drivers will automatically improve their habits when they know that you can see how they drive. A driver might not even realize how much money could be saved just by changing how they accelerate and brake, and when they do, they know they are helping the company waste less, which is going to come back to them too. Same for truckers. Fatigue puts a driver at risk and with GPS tracking you can be sure your drivers are getting rest. The technology gets everyone involved so your driver doesn’t push themself past their optimum condition and knows you’re on their side.

Save Money On Fleet Maintenance

How about cutting costs with servicing and maintenance? GPS tracking can help you with vehicle service reminders in many ways.

Set up maintenance based on mileage. Set an alert by miles driven, engine hours or days. You get the maintenance done when it’s needed, not too early and certainly not when it’s too late. Consistently keeping a vehicle in good running order is going to save you money. Sure the driver can keep an eye on it – but why not monitor and manage the whole fleet with GPS tracking? Track parts, vendors, service histories. This is a very very scalable solution.

But why stop with just that. We can help you monitor a deeper level of data in terms of the condition of the engine and on-board diagnostics, so you have a real-time picture of the state of your vehicle and the ability to detect and handle problems proactively. That’s right – you can see when vital repairs are needed by alerts and get them fixed fast.

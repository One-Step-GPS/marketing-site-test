Careers

Build your career with a great team, in a fast-growing tech company

We are always looking to expand our team with knowledgeable and self-motivated employees. Our greatest growth is still ahead of us.

We offer great benefits: excellent pay, unlimited bonus potential, and healthcare benefits.

If you're interested in joining the team, please send an email with a resume to hiring@onestepgps.com, and we'll set up an interview when we have an opening that can make use of your unique skill set.

What we do

One Step GPS is focused on helping companies change and improve the way they do business and service their customers. Companies using our solutions see increased productivity, reduced fuel costs, elimination of unauthorized trips, side jobs, unneeded overtime and much more.

With incredibly feature-rich and affordable GPS solutions deployed around the world, we are spearheading change in this industry by offering our service without contracts or equipment fees at a fraction of the cost of major competitors. We are expanding at an amazing rate, and the growth continues to accelerate.

What it's like to work for us

You will be given intensive and in depth training. You will then get to apprentice under some of the top business development representatives, learn the talk and walk the walk. Every representative of our company learns how to help their customers achieve success. You’ll be supporting the same customers you sign up, and helping them expand the tracking of their fleet as they grow. This is meant to make sure you succeed, make great money and have fun while doing it.

Our office has Nerf guns, optional treadmill desks, weekly company lunches, tons of snacks and unlimited coffee. We have a corporate health insurance plan with employer contribution and paid sick time. Our schedule is family-oriented with no weekends or night shifts. With that said, this is not some start-up out of the TV show Silicon Valley with tons of extra staff who don’t know what to do. Every employee comes to work ready to make things happen and makes things happen for the entire time they’re at work.

Why build your career with us

If you’ve ever dreamed of earning an excellent income working for an industry leader, while helping businesses around the world small and big, then you’ve found the right place. As we continue to grow in this rapidly expanding industry, so do our administrative, managerial, and executive positions. We like to promote from within, and reward our most productive employees, so the opportunity for growth and advancement is unlimited.

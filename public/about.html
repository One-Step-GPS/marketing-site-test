About

In the beginning...

Several years ago, our sister company, which managed a huge fleet of vehicles, was an early adopter of GPS tracking.
The benefits were immediately evident. The devices improved the ability to manage all employees from a single office, without having to worry about the accuracy of employees self-reporting their locations.

In addition, dispatching the closest vehicle to each appointment became a breeze. They no longer had to rely on memory or call numerous drivers to find out who was available and who was the closest.

With a quick look on a map and the click of a button, once onerous tasks became simple. This increased the speed of service, overall efficiency and most importantly, customer satisfaction. Needless to say, profits went up and the solution paid for itself many times over.

The high cost of poor technology

At the time, the GPS tracking industry was dominated by a handful of companies who insisted on ironclad contracts, hefty cancellation fees and significant upfront costs for equipment. This meant that costs were prohibitive for many businesses.

The GPS tracking devices themselves were often not up to scratch. The company providing the tracking service had a three-day outage and didn’t seem to care. To make matters worse, shortly thereafter, all their devices stopped working. Our sister company was then asked to upgrade from 2G devices to 3G and pay for new installation. The costs just kept going up.

At that point, the idea of revolutionizing the industry became a new goal. We figured there must be a better way and, within a few years, One Step GPS was born.

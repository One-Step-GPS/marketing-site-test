Benefits of using One Step GPS

The benefits of using ELD
ELD improves the speed of record-keeping, reduces human error, and eliminates the need to stop and keep manual reports of odometer readings and miles driven in each state. Benefits include easier scheduling between fleet managers and drivers, more accurate overtime and payroll records, and increased efficiency in vehicle inspection and reports, to name a few.
How it works
With the simple installation of our device, you can pair the driver's phone or tablet and get started. Our app is easy to use and keeps up to date with all the regulations and exemptions - making it easy for your fleet to stay compliant. ELDs communicate with a truck's engine control systems to collect VIN, engine status, RPMs, speed, engine hours, as well as provide accurate, near real-time GPS location information to a logging device or app that DOT officials can check when needed.
